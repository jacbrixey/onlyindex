What is Choctaw?

Choctaw is a Muskogean language spoken by several hundred members of the Choctaw Nation of Oklahoma and by several thousand members of the Mississippi Band of Choctaw Indians.


Choctaw alphabet

The Choctaw alphabet contains the following letters:

a b c f h i k l m n o p s t w y


Vowels can take accent marks

á a̱ í i̱ ó o̱
